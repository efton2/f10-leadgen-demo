Yes — And It Is More Powerful Than Any Sales Script

Three ways to deploy the demo. All three use the same tool. Pick the one that fits your style.

Picture This

You walk into a dental office. The receptionist says "Can I help you?" You say: "I was in the area — I noticed your business came up in my AI system. Can I show the doctor something real quick? Takes 90 seconds."

You open a tablet. Their name, their reviews, their hours, their services — all loaded. You press play. An AI voice agent starts talking about their practice in a real conversation. The doctor has never seen anything like this. You have not said a word about price yet.

A

Walk-In (In-Person)

You or Sabrina physically walk into target businesses in your area. Pull up the demo on a tablet or laptop. Show them their own business in the system — live, on the spot. The shock factor alone closes rooms. No cold call, no email campaign. Just show up with their data already loaded.

Best for: Dentists, HVAC, salons, law firms — any local business with a physical location. High close rate. Slower volume.

B

Personalized Video (Loom / Screen Record)

Record a 90-second screen share showing their business loaded in the demo. Send via email or LinkedIn DM with the subject line: "I built something using your business — 90 seconds." They watch it on their own time. ACE follows up automatically if they do not respond.

Best for: Remote outreach at scale. 10–20 videos a day. Works anywhere, not just local.

C

Live Demo Link (Send and Let ACE Close)

Search their business in the lead gen tool, copy their unique demo link, and send it via email, DM, or text with a single line: "Built this for your business — take a look." They click, see their own data, interact with the AI. ACE follows up if they engage but do not book. Fully automated after the send.

Best for: Maximum scale. 50+ outreaches a day. Lowest touch, highest volume.

My recommendation: Start with walk-ins for the first 5–10 clients. You need real closes, real objections, and real feedback to tighten the pitch. Then switch to video outreach at scale once you know what lands. Use ACE for all follow-up from day one regardless of which approach you use.
